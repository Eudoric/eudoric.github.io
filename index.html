🌙 Eudoria Lunar Calendar

13 Moons, 13 Months, 28 Days Each = 364 Days

A sacred lunar calendar marking the cycles and celebrations of Eudoria

🌿 Eudorasis

Month 1 • Spring

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Day 1: Bloomday

Eudoria's New Year and the anniversary of the world's birth. The day when the world Eudoria was born (formerly known as Eudran before its transformation).

Duration: 1 day

🌸 Primoria

Month 2 • Spring

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Day 14: Nature Day

A mid-spring celebration of nature's renewal and growth.

Duration: 1 day

Day 21: AY'MOR - The Day of Love

A celebration of love and connection among the people of Eudoria.

Duration: 1 day

🌻 Sera

Month 3 • Spring

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Day 10: Passover

A sacred seven-day observance during the third month.

Duration: 7 days (10th-17th)

🔥 Maunox

Month 4 • Summer

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Day 7: Mauifesta

A celebration of Maui's seven legendary deeds and his role as a protector and leader in Polynesian mythology.

Duration: 8 days (7th-14th)

🌊 Naimara

Month 5 • Summer

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Jouvert de Naime (Naime Fete) (Full Month)

A local celebration during the rainy season of Naimara, a festival of joy and community.

Duration: Variable (rainy season)

☀️ Afronox

Month 6 • Summer

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

No major holidays this month.

🍂 Eudorine

Month 7 • Autumn

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Day 7: Noga Day

A celebration of the First Family of the heartland.

Duration: 1 day

📖 Suliamun

Month 8 • Autumn

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

No major holidays this month.

🍁 Naaviemun

Month 9 • Autumn

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Fasting to Barak Festival (Full Month)

A 12-day fasting period in preparation for the Barak Festival. The exact dates shift based on lunar phases.

Duration: 12 days (dates vary by lunar phase)

Day 27: GRAZEORA - The Day of Feast

A grand feast celebrating abundance and community bonds.

Duration: 2 days (27th-28th)

🍁 Kanythos

Month 10 • Autumn/Early Winter

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Day 28: Soulfall — The Night When the Veil Breathes

The last night before winter. When the Creator lets the souls fall. A sacred time when the veil between worlds is thin.

Duration: 1 night

❄️ Zendariyah

Month 11 • Winter

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

No major holidays this month.

🎁 Afrialuna

Month 12 • Winter

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Day 22: The Three Days of Kinlight

A winter celebration of kinship and light during the darkest season.

Duration: 3 days (22nd-24th)

🌙 Yahuahor

Month 13 • Winter

Days: 1-28 (Lunar month)

📅 Holidays & Special Days

Yaum Al-Tahrir (Day of Liberation) (Full Month)

An entire moon cycle of celebration marking liberation and renewal.

Duration: Full month (28 days)
